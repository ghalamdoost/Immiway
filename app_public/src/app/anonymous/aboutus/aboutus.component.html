About Us

 

Mina Yousefi Immigration Service Inc. (MYIS)

Mina Yousefi Immigration Services is an immigration firm that is proud to cater to the needs of immigrants from all over the world.

Mina Yousefi Immigration Services has been in practice, specializing in immigration law for the past 15 years.

By choosing Mina Yousefi Immigration Services, you receive a high level of professional care and attention that gives you peace of mind knowing that our team is by your side, every step of the way.

Our firm offers all Canadian immigration programs as well as second residency and citizenship programs in a global sense.

We are specialized in Business and Investment immigration not only to Canada but also to the USA, European and Caribbean countries. The headquarter of Easy Way Immigration Services Inc. is in Toronto, Canada and the company has offices in Iran with expert staff, including lawyers and RCICs (Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant) who are all dedicated to assisting you in transforming your dream into the reality of a new home in any country you desire.

 Founder

Mina Yousefi is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC), and a member in good standing of the ICCRC (Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council) with membership ID, R421879. She had held the title of President of the Iranian-Canadian Association of Immigration Consultants for 2 years and was the head of the education committee for 3 years. The said association provides CPD, Continuing Professional Development to RCIC members in form of educational seminars, workshops, or conferences.

Mina Yousefi is also an education advisor who is helping thousands of students from different countries pursue their dream of studying in Canada, the U.S. and European Countries.

Mina Yousefi holds an Immigration Consultancy Graduate Certificate with honours from Seneca College in Toronto as well as a Bachelor of Business Management. Mina Yousefi has served as an account manager at Royal Bank of Canada for many years before starting an immigration practice, as a former banker and mutual fund representative at the most distinguished bank of Canada, she is well acquainted with Canadian financial system regulations. Mina Yousefi has helped hundreds of investors and entrepreneurs from around the world to start new companies or open their company branches in Canada.

Mina Yousefi brings over 20 years of specialized immigration, business, and investment experience to the firm.
